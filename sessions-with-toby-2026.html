Try This Right Now (2 Minutes)

This is the "Cry" exercise. It bypasses the "trying harder" reflex.

1
Think of something sad. A loss. A goodbye. Feel the "sob" in your chest.
2
Say "Wah" — like a baby crying. Don't sing it. Speak it with that sad feeling.
3
Sing "Wah" on a scale: do-re-mi-fa-sol-fa-mi-re-do
4
Notice the shift. The high notes feel lighter. That's your mix voice engaging.
⏱ 2 minutes · Do this 3x daily for 1 week
