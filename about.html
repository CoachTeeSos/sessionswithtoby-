About

I Help People Become.

Not become someone else. Become who they already are. Here's how that started.

The Story

It Started With a Seven-Year-Old

A kid was struggling in school. Everyone said: "Make him study harder." But I watched — and I saw something else. He wasn't lazy. He was lost.

So instead of forcing academics, I found what lit him up. Built around that. Two weeks later, grades went up. Not because he studied more. Because he had a reason to.

That's when I understood: coaching isn't about fixing. It's about finding.

The Journey

How We Got Here

2018
The First Student

Started informal vocal coaching. One church singer at a time. Word spread.

2020
Going Online

Pandemic forced the move online. Turns out, WhatsApp calls work just fine. Students from different countries started showing up.

2022
Life Coaching

Students kept asking: "Can you help with the other stuff too?" Life coaching became official.

2024
50+ Students

Crossed 50 students across 10+ countries. Still hands-on. Still personal.

2026
To Be Come

Built a full coaching ecosystem: quiz, community, group and 1-on-1. The mission stays the same — help people become.
