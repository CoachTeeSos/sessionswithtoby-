The Story

It Started With a Seven-Year-Old

A kid was struggling in school. Everyone said: "Make him study harder." But I watched — and I saw something else. He wasn't lazy. He was lost.

So instead of forcing academics, I found what lit him up. Built around that. Two weeks later, grades went up. Not because he studied more. Because he had a reason to.

That's when I understood: coaching isn't about fixing. It's about finding.

My name is Prosper Tobi Olumo — known to most as Coach Toby or CoachTeeSos. I'm a Nigerian vocal coach and life coach based in Abuja. I work with singers, performers, worship leaders, and creators who are done staying the same.

I didn't start out as a vocal coach. I started out as someone who could see what was holding people back — and who refused to let them stay there. That instinct turned into a coaching practice that has now served 50+ students across 10+ countries, from Lagos to London, Abuja to Toronto.

My approach is simple but not easy: find the thing that lights you up, build everything around it, and don't settle for less than what you're capable of. Whether that's hitting a high note you've been avoiding for five years, commanding a room during a presentation, or figuring out what the next chapter of your life actually looks like — the process is the same. Discover. Build. Become.

What makes my coaching different? I don't do factory coaching. I don't do one-size-fits-all programs where you're just a number. When you work with me, you get me — all in. I'll push you because I see what you can become, not because I'm harsh. There's a difference.

I've been singing and performing for years. I know what it's like to stand on stage and feel your voice crack under pressure. I know what it's like to wonder if you're good enough. And I know what it's like to have someone believe in your voice before you did — and how that changes everything.

That's why I built Sessions with Toby — a full coaching ecosystem that includes a vocal quiz to identify your exact bottleneck, a free singers' community for daily accountability, group coaching for team environments, and 1-on-1 sessions for deep transformation. Every piece is designed to help you become who you already are.

I'm also pivoting into medicine — currently studying Biomedical Sciences at the University of Ottawa. Because the voice is an instrument, and I want to understand it from the inside out. Anatomy, physiology, the science of sound — it all feeds back into how I coach.

If you're reading this, you're already closer than most people get. The fact that you're here means something. Take the quiz. Book a session. Join the community. Or just say hello. I respond to every message.
