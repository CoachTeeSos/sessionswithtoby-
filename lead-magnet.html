5 Vocal Exercises That
Instantly Improve Your Tone

Even if you've been singing wrong for years.

How to use this guide: Do all 5 exercises in order, every day, for 10 minutes total. No equipment needed. No warm-up required — these ARE the warm-up. After 2 weeks, your tone will be noticeably fuller, more connected, and more confident.
Exercise 1

The Hum Slide

Hum gently on "mmm" — from your lowest comfortable note to your highest, then back down. Keep it light. Feel the vibration move from your chest to your face.

How: 2 minutes. Slow and steady. Don't push — let the sound find its own path.

💡 Why it works: Connects your chest and head voice without strain. Most singers have a "break" between the two — this smooths it out.

Exercise 2

The Lip Trill Scale

Blow air through loose lips (like a horse) while singing a 5-note scale. If your lips stop trilling, you're pushing too hard.

How: 3 rounds, ascending by half-step each time. Keep it bouncy and relaxed.

💡 Why it works: Forces proper breath support and eliminates throat tension. Your lips regulate the air so your throat can't tighten up.

Exercise 3

The "Ng" Exercise

Sing "ng" (like the end of "sing") on a comfortable note. Feel the resonance buzz in your nasal cavity and cheekbones — that's "forward placement."

How: Hold each note for 5 seconds. 5 reps. Focus on the buzz in your face, not your throat.

💡 Why it works: Places your voice in the "mask" — the resonant space that makes your tone sound full and professional instead of thin and breathy.

Exercise 4

The Siren on "Wee"

Slide from your lowest note to your highest on "wee" — like a siren. Let it crack and break naturally. Don't force a smooth transition.

How: 5 reps, slow and controlled. Glide, don't jump.

💡 Why it works: Maps your full range and teaches your voice to transition between registers without the dreaded "crack."

Exercise 5

The Whisper Sing

Sing your favorite song at full pitch but whisper volume. Keep the tone placement correct even though you're barely making sound.

How: 3 minutes with any song you know well. Whisper the words but sing the notes.

💡 Why it works: Builds muscle memory for proper placement without any strain. Your brain learns the right position without your throat getting in the way.

🎵 The Rule

Do all 5 exercises in order. 10 minutes total. Every day for 2 weeks.
Your tone will change. I've seen it happen dozens of times.
