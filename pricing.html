FAQ

Questions Before You Commit

How does the Session 1 guarantee work?

After your first session, if you genuinely don't hear a measurable difference in your voice, you don't pay. I'll refund you or let the session go — no questions asked. But I've never had to. The method works.

Can I pay in Naira with my Nigerian card?

Yes. Flutterwave handles Naira payments automatically. Just tap any plan and pay — your card works. No stress.

What happens after I pay?

You'll get a WhatsApp message within 5 minutes. We introduce ourselves, discuss your goals, and book your first session — usually within 48 hours.

What if I need to reschedule?

24 hours notice = free reschedule, no questions. Life happens. I get it.

Do you only coach singers?

Vocal coaching is the core. But I also do life coaching (ages 18–28, direction/purpose/focus) and public speaking. The custom plan lets you mix all three.

Is this an ongoing commitment?

Single session = no commitment at all. Monthly = cancel whenever you're done. The community = join/leave anytime. Zero lock-in.
