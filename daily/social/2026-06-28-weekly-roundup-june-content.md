Lesson 1: Fatigue isn't weakness — it's a signal.

Your voice getting tired after 10 minutes doesn't mean you're broken. It means there's tension squeezing efficiency out of every note. A 90-second hard reset can add 20 minutes of clean practice to your session.

**Tweet 3:**
Lesson 2: Comparison is measuring the wrong thing.

Your Tuesday bathroom practice vs their studio highlight reel? That's chapter 3 vs chapter 20. The data isn't even valid. Compare to your past self. That's the only metric that motivates.

**Tweet 4:**
Lesson 3: Consistency beats intensity. Every. Single. Time.

7 minutes daily > 2 hours once a week. Neural pathways don't care about effort — they care about frequency. Seven minutes counts. It always counted.

**Tweet 5:**
Lesson 4: Your emotions about singing shape your voice physically.

Anxiety tightens. Resistance blocks. Comparison crushes. Shift how you feel about practice and your voice responds differently. The technique is only half the story.

Weekly recording. 7-minute minimum. Comparison reset. Confidence is a byproduct of showing up so many times that competence becomes undeniable.
