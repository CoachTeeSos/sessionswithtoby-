The Code

How This Community Actually Works

No fluff. No filler. Just the rules that make this room different.

🎵

Stay Open

Bring an open mind. Let your expectations be the floor, not the ceiling. The best version of you is the one that shows up willing to be surprised.

🎵

Drop the Mask

Be honest and less performative. Save the "acting" for the stage — we value the real you here. 🤣 No curated personas. Just raw, unfiltered musicians figuring it out together.

🎵

Social Responsibility

Respect and understanding are our core values. We are building a culture, not just a group chat. Every voice here matters — including yours.

📍

Stay Active

You must acknowledge community messages with a response or reaction. (Must eh!) This isn't a passive community. If you're here, you're here.

💡

Suggestions Welcome

Got an idea to make us better? Message the Admins. Pitch your suggestions persuasively and intuitively — we're always listening.
