The Code

How this collective actually works. No fluff.

🎵

Stay Open

Bring an open mind. Let your expectations be the floor, not the ceiling. The best version of you shows up when you're willing to be surprised.

🎵

Drop the Mask

Be honest. Be less performative. Save the acting for the stage — we value the real you here. No curated personas. Just musicians figuring it out together.

🎵

Social Responsibility

Respect and understanding are non-negotiable. We're building a culture, not just a group. Every voice here matters — including yours.

📍

Stay Active

Acknowledge messages. React. Respond. Show up. (Must eh!) This isn't a passive collective. If you're here, you're here.

Suggestions Welcome
