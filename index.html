About

From Struggling Singer
to World-Class Mentor

Coach Toby's journey from failed auditions to transforming thousands of voices across 15 countries.

2015
The Beginning
Started vocal training in Lagos with zero resources but an unshakable belief that every voice matters. Borrowed a neighbor's keyboard to practice.
🎤
2017
Biggest Failure
Lost his voice completely during a 3-month period. Couldn't sing, couldn't teach. Hit rock bottom. This darkness became the seedbed for the vocal training method.
🌑
2019
The Breakthrough
Developed a vocal training method combining vocal science with spiritual understanding.erstanding. First student went from tone-deaf to worship leader in 6 months.
