About

From Struggling Singer
to World-Class Mentor

Coach Toby's journey from failed auditions to transforming thousands of voices across 15 countries.

2015
The Beginning
Started vocal training in Lagos with zero resources but an unshakable belief that every voice matters.
2017
Biggest Failure
Lost his voice completely during a 3-month period. Couldn't sing, couldn't teach. Hit rock bottom.
2019
The Breakthrough
Developed the NYVC method combining vocal science with spiritual understanding. First student went from tone-deaf to worship leader in 6 months.
2021
Going Global
Launched SessionswithToby online. Within 12 months: 500+ students across Nigeria, UK, USA, and Cameroon.
2024
The Mission
2,400+ students later, the mission remains the same: help every singer unlock the voice they were born with.
