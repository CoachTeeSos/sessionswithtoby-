The Guarantee

The Session 1 Guarantee applies to the Single Session ($50) and the first session of the Monthly Package ($200). If, after attending a full 60-minute session, you do not hear a measurable difference in your voice as assessed by the coach, you will not be charged. This is not a loophole. This is not a marketing trick. This is a craftsman standing behind his work.

What "Measurable Difference" Means

It means what it sounds like. You will hear your voice do something it couldn't do before. A note you couldn't hit. A crack that's gone. A resonance you didn't know you had. This is assessed by the coach — a professional with 50+ students across 10+ countries — not by your feelings, your mood, or your mother's opinion. If the coach says you're different, you're different. If he says you're not, you don't pay.

Why These Terms Exist

Not to protect me from you. To protect you from yourself.

Most people who read terms and conditions before booking a vocal coach are the same people who will find a reason it "didn't work" — not because it didn't, but because admitting it worked means admitting they should have started sooner. That's a hard pill. I understand.

These terms exist so that the person who shows up ready — the one who actually wants to become — has a clear path. No ambiguity. No "but you said." Just: did your voice change or didn't it?

The Philosophy

I have never had to pay this guarantee out. Not once. Not with 50 students. Not across 10 countries. Not with beginners who couldn't hold a note. Not with "experienced" singers who'd been doing it wrong for years.

Why? Because the work is real. The method is real. And your voice — your actual, God-given voice — is not the problem. It never was.

The guarantee is not a safety net. It's a mirror. It says: I trust my craft more than I trust your doubt.

If you're the kind of person who reads this far into the terms and conditions of a vocal coaching website — you're overthinking it. Book the session. Your voice is waiting.
