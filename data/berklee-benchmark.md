## Level Benchmarks
- Beginner: controlled onset, steady appoggio, basic posture/breath, intro theory/ear training.
- Intermediate: mix/belt coordination, rhythmic accuracy, call/response, stage craft.
- Advanced: rasps/distortion control, register balance, performance stamina, song analysis.
- Professional: studio/performance consistency, coaching/brand readiness.

## Assessment Requirements
- Knowledge checks per module with passing score ≥ 80%.
