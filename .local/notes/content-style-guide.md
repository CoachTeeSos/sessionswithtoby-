# Justin Stoney (NYVC) Content Approach — Reference for Toby

## Core Principles
1. **Practical first** — Every exercise has a clear, numbered step-by-step process
2. **"Why it works"** — Always explain the mechanism behind the exercise
3. **Relatable analogies** — Compare vocal concepts to everyday experiences
4. **Video-first mindset** — Content should be demonstrable, not just describable
5. **Quick tips + deep dives** — Mix 2-minute exercises with 20-minute masterclasses
6. **Address the singer directly** — "You" language, not "singers should"
7. **Name the problem first** — Identify the struggle before offering the solution
8. **Confidence-building tone** — Encouraging but technically precise

## Exercise Format (NYVC Style)
- **Name of exercise**
- **What it fixes** (the problem)
- **How to do it** (numbered steps)
- **What to feel/notice** (sensory feedback)
- **Why it works** (the science/simple explanation)
- **Pro tip** (advanced note)

## Content Types
- Quick Singing Tips (2-3 min)
- Voice Lessons To The World (Q&A format)
- Exercise demonstrations (5-10 min)
- Common mistakes breakdowns
- Artist-specific technique tutorials

## Tone
- Professional but warm
- Technically accurate but accessible
- Encouraging without being patronizing
- Direct — no fluff
