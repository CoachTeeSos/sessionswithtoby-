Life Coaching vs Therapy: Which One Do You Need?

Clear explanation of the difference between life coaching and therapy. When to choose each. By CoachToby, certified life coach.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can a life coach help with anxiety?

A life coach can help with goal-setting and confidence around anxiety, but cannot treat clinical anxiety. For diagnosed conditions, see a therapist. Many people work with both.

How do I know if I need life coaching?

You need coaching if you feel stuck, lack direction, struggle with confidence, want accountability, or need help setting and achieving goals. Coaching is forward-focused.

What happens in a life coaching session?

Sessions typically last 45-60 minutes. You discuss your goals, challenges, and progress. The coach asks powerful questions, helps you create action plans, and holds you accountable.
