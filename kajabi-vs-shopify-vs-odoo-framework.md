# Kajabi vs Shopify vs Odoo — Lead Generation Framework for Coaching

## The Core Question
How do these platforms generate leads, and how can you replicate their best parts for your vocal/life coaching business?

---

## 1. KAJABI — The Coaching Machine

### How Kajabi does lead gen:
```
LANDING PAGE → LEAD MAGNET (freebie) → EMAIL CAPTURE → NURTURE SEQUENCE → OFFER → SALE
```

**Key components:**
- **Landing pages** — drag-and-drop builder, no code needed
- **Lead magnets** — free PDF, video, mini-course, or challenge in exchange for email
- **Email automation** — built-in sequences (welcome → value → pitch → follow-up)
- **Pipelines** — visual funnel builder connecting pages + emails + offers
- **Community** — built-in membership area for students
- **Payments** — Stripe/PayPal integration for one-time or recurring payments
- **Affiliate system** — others can promote your program for commission

**Kajabi's secret sauce:** Everything is in one place. No integrations needed. The landing page, email, payment, and course delivery are all native.

### Replica for your coaching business:
| Kajabi Feature | Your Replica |
|---|---|
| Landing page | Build with HTML/CSS (dark theme) or Carrd.co ($19/yr) |
| Lead magnet | Free "5-Day Vocal Warmup Guide" PDF or video |
| Email automation | MailerLite (free up to 1K subs) or Brevo (you already have this) |
| Pipeline | Google Sheets pipeline (Lead → Contacted → Trial → Paid) |
| Community | WhatsApp group or Telegram |
| Payments | Flutterwave (you already have it integrated) |
| Affiliate | Manual — give students a referral code, track in Airtable |

## 2. SHOPIFY — The Product Engine

### How Shopify does lead gen:
```
PRODUCT PAGE → EMAIL POPUP → DISCOUNT OFFER → ABANDONED CART EMAIL → RETARGETING ADS → SALE
```

**Key components:**
- **Product pages** — optimized for conversion with reviews, urgency, social proof
- **Email popups** — "Get 10% off your first order" in exchange for email
- **Abandoned cart** — automated emails when someone doesn't complete purchase
- **Discount codes** — time-limited offers to create urgency
- **Blog** — content marketing to drive organic traffic
- **Apps ecosystem** — 8000+ apps for reviews, upsells, email, etc.

**Shopify's secret sauce:** The abandoned cart email alone recovers 10-15% of lost sales. Urgency and scarcity drive conversions.

### What's useful for coaching:
| Shopify Tactic | How to Apply |
|---|---|
| Email popup with discount | "Book a free vocal assessment" popup on your site |
| Abandoned cart | If someone starts booking but doesn't finish → send WhatsApp reminder |
| Urgency/scarcity | "Only 5 spots left this month" on your booking page |
| Social proof | Testimonials from your 9 current students on your landing page |
| Blog/content | "How to improve your vocal range" articles to drive traffic |

## 3. ODOO — The Business Suite

### How Odoo does lead gen:
```
WEBSITE → CRM PIPELINE → EMAIL CAMPAIGNS → INVOICING → AUTOMATION → SALE
```

**Key components:**
- **CRM** — visual kanban pipeline (New → Qualified → Proposition → Won)
- **Website builder** — drag-and-drop with lead capture forms
- **Email campaigns** — template-based, track open/click rates
- **Automation** — trigger actions based on lead behavior (e.g., if lead opens email 3 times → send offer)
- **Invoicing** — professional invoices, recurring billing
- **Reporting** — dashboards showing conversion rates, revenue, lead sources

**Odoo's secret sauce:** The CRM + automation combo. You can set up rules like "If a lead hasn't been contacted in 3 days, send a reminder email" or "When a lead books a call, move them to 'Qualified' automatically."

### Replica for your coaching business:
| Odoo Feature | Your Replica |
|---|---|
| CRM Pipeline | Airtable (you already have this) — add pipeline stages |
| Website | Your existing sessionswithtoby site |
| Email campaigns | Brevo or MailerLite |
| Automation | Zapier or n8n (free) to connect Airtable → Email → WhatsApp |
| Invoicing | Wave (free) or Paystack invoices |
| Reporting | Google Sheets dashboard pulling from Airtable |

## THE WINNING FRAMEWORK — Combine All 3

Here's the lead gen system built from the best parts of each:

### STAGE 1: ATTRACT (Kajabi + Shopify)
- Landing page with a free lead magnet: **"Free 5-Day Vocal Challenge"** or **"NYVC Method Starter Guide"**
- Content marketing: Short YouTube/TikTok videos showing vocal tips
- Social proof: Testimonials from current students

### STAGE 2: CAPTURE (All 3)
- Email signup form on landing page
- WhatsApp CTA: "Text START to [your number] for the free guide"
- Popup offering the lead magnet in exchange for name + email

### STAGE 3: NURTURE (Kajabi + Odoo)
- **Day 1:** Welcome email + deliver lead magnet
- **Day 2:** Your story — why you coach, your method
- **Day 3:** Student success story / testimonial
- **Day 4:** Free value — a quick vocal tip video
- **Day 5:** The offer — book a free discovery call or enroll in coaching

### STAGE 4: CONVERT (Shopify + Odoo)
- Limited spots: "I only take 10 students per month"
- Urgency: "Enrollment closes Friday"
- Easy payment: Paystack link or Flutterwave
- Abandonment follow-up: If they don't complete payment → WhatsApp reminder

### STAGE 5: RETAIN (Kajabi)
- Community: WhatsApp group for all students
- Weekly check-ins (you already do this)
- Upsell: Monthly coaching → Group coaching → Advanced program
