About

It started with a seven-year-old who was struggling in school. Everyone said "make him study harder." But I saw something else — he wasn't lazy, he was lost. So instead of forcing academics, I found what lit him up and built around that. Two weeks later, grades went up. Not because he studied more. Because he had a reason to.

That's when I understood: coaching isn't about fixing. It's about finding.

Today, I've served 50+ students across Lagos, London, Abuja, Toronto, and beyond. My approach is simple but not easy — find the thing that lights you up, build everything around it, and don't settle for less than what you're capable of.

I built Sessions with Toby — a full coaching ecosystem that includes a vocal quiz to identify your exact bottleneck, a free singers' community, group coaching, and 1-on-1 sessions for deep transformation.
